What is Christmas?

Christmas is a joyous annual celebration that marks the birth of Jesus Christ, celebrated on December 25 with fervor and festivity across the globe. It is a cherished religious and cultural observance, bringing together billions of people who engage in various traditions, customs, and festivities to honor the significance of this special day. Christmas is a time of love, generosity, and togetherness, where families and communities unite to share in the spirit of giving, reflecting on the message of hope and goodwill that this festive season symbolizes for people of diverse backgrounds and beliefs.
